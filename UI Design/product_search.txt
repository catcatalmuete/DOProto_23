Misc. Product Search Functionality

- sorting by price range & location

- "no result" message for searches that turn up empty

- items will be manually or automatically classified into general categories to narrow searches

- keyword search for more specific searches, and potential to include autocomplete in search bar as a further feature

- related item recommendations based on products users engage with, purchase, or save

- implementation of sponsored items, like on Amazon, to visually incentivize certain results of different search filters at a cost

- database to handle user accounts, which can maintain saved products and possibly user preferences set in settings in an attempt to personalize search experience as much as possible
